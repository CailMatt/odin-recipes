Garlic Butter Pasta

Description:

This recipes is super simple and cheap!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz of pasta (any kind)
  • 3 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 3 Cloves Minced Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesean Cheese to taste

    • Steps:

    1. Cook the pasta: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package directions, then drain.
    2. Make the garlic butter sauce: While the pasta cooks, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant (don't let it burn!).
    3. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it with the garlic butter sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
    4. Add cheese and garnish (optional): Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the pasta and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
